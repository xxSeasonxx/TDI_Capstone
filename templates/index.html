Data and Motivation

New York City, as one of the most prosperous cities on the planet, has almost 400 years of history. Numerous problems arise on a daily basis and perplex the living environment for local residents. In response, New Yorkers can file complaints to the New York authorities and ask for resolutions, and this is known as the 311 services requests line. However, not all of your complaints will be responsibly resolved by the NY authorities (NYA) and thus your issue may grow and become severe. My website aims to help user foresee their complains result and let them make a ratical decision.

Enormous Demand

High demand is the driving reason. Now day New York has 8.5 million population, and in 2015 one year alone, there were 2.5 million complaints were filed. Meaning about 1 in 4 of us depends on this service and awaits for New York authorities resolutions.

Authorities

Each complaint is followed by a New York authority's resolution, and I have data begin 2011 till now. With this unique dataset, I am able to investigate and evaluate the performance of NYA. Determine if NYA has made their effort to resolve a problem to its root.

Ask the Right Question

The ultimate purpose is to improve our city and to better our city we ought to differentiate the problems. That is asking the right question. Why there is a complaint happening? What is the fundamental problem behind a complaint? Using machine learning, we can distinguish the critical aspects of a complaint.
